Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Description

This recipe is my own take of Shrimp Scampi which alters some ingredients and steps to minimize time. White wine is sometimes used for the sauce but wont be used for this recipe. All the preperation for the recipe can be done while other steps are in progress, making the total time of the dish under 20 min.

Ingredients

  • shrimp(deveined)
  • lemon
  • 1 clove garlic for every 2 shrimp
  • olive oil
  • red chili flakes
  • pasta - preferably thin and noodle
  • butter
  • salt & pepper
  • cayenne powder
  • parsley
  • parmesan

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil with a generous amount of salt. Add pasta and cook for however long the box instructs to.

  2. While pasta boils, prep other ingredients - mince garlic, chop up the parsely, and season shrimp with salt, pepper, and cayenne.

  3. Saute the shrimp in a pan with some olive oil over medium heat. Cook both sides for around 2 min before removing from pan.

  4. Add some more olive oil to the pan. Add in the minced garlic and however much red pepper flakes. Turn heat to medium low and carefully watch garlic for burning.

  5. Once fragrant, add in majority of the parsely, a couple knobs of butter, a squeeze of lemon, and a ladle full of the pasta water. Let all the garlic oil emulsify with the butter and pasta water until the consistency becomes thicker.

  6. Around this point, the pasta should have finished cooking. Add the pasta alongside the rest of the shrimp. Toss the pasta around in the sauce and season to taste.

  7. Plate and garnish with the rest of the parsley as a well as a generous amount of parmesan.
